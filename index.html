CodeX is a global community of healthcare stakeholders currently working on data standards in three domains.

Join Us and Spread the Word

Cancer

The first domain that the CodeX community tackled is cancer. Using the minimal Common Oncology Data Elements (mCODE), an open data standard enabling enabled high-quality data capture for cancer that can be widely adopted.

70+ oncology definitions in use at Dana Farber, MD Anderson, Mass General Hospital and 44 other institutions. Versions of the mCODE and associated modeling tools are currently operational in over 250 organizations
